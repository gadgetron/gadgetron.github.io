The Gadgetron is an open source framework for medical image reconstruction. It has been developed at the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute, NIH, Bethesda, MD, USA and at the Department of Computer Science and Department of Clinical Medicine, Aarhus University, Denmark. It is made freely available to the medical image reconstruction community.

The main portal for access to source code, documentation, etc. is the GitHub website. Please direct any questions to our Discourse Group.

If you find the Gadgetron useful in your research, please cite these papers:

Hansen MS, Sørensen TS. Gadgetron: An Open Source Framework for Medical Image Reconstruction. Magn Reson Med. 2013 Jun;69(6):1768-76.

Xue H, Inati S, Sørensen TS, Kellman P, Hansen MS. Distributed MRI Reconstruction Using Gadgetron-Based Cloud Computing. Magn Reson Med. 2015 Mar;73(3):1015<80><93>1025.
